## Full Description

MyTaxReturn AU helps individuals estimate their Australian tax return outcome for the supported financial year.

The app guides you through income, deductions, Medicare details, HELP debt, PAYG withholding, private hospital cover, spouse income, and dependents. It then shows an estimate of income tax, offsets, Medicare levy, Medicare levy surcharge, HELP repayment, tax withheld, and expected refund or amount owing.

Key features:

- Australian resident tax estimates for the supported financial year
- Guided income, ABN/business income, deduction, Medicare, HELP, and private health inputs
- Repayment-income fields for HELP estimates
- Family and spouse income handling for Medicare levy and Medicare levy surcharge estimates
- Saved calculations and scenario comparison
- PDF/share report support for personal records
- Source-linked tax assumptions and visible calculation disclaimers

Important:

MyTaxReturn AU is an estimate calculator only. It is not affiliated with the Australian Taxation Office and is not operated by a registered tax agent. It does not lodge tax returns, provide personal tax advice, or guarantee a refund or tax outcome. For final tax decisions, use official ATO services or consult a registered tax agent.

## Review Notes

This app is an Australian personal tax estimate calculator. It does not lodge a tax return, connect to myGov, connect to ATO systems, request a TFN, request identity documents, request bank details, or provide registered tax agent services. Saved calculations are stored locally on the device. Analytics and crash reporting are environment-controlled and should not send raw tax amounts or identity details.
