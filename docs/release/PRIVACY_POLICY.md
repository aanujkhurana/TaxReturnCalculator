# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 June 2026

MyTaxReturn AU is an Australian tax estimate calculator. This policy explains what data the app stores, what may leave the device, and what controls are available.

## Data You Enter

The app lets you enter tax-estimate information such as income amounts, deductions, Medicare details, HELP debt status, spouse/family details, private hospital cover details, and tax withheld amounts.

Do not enter tax file numbers, identity document numbers, bank account details, passwords, myGov credentials, or any other identity document details into this app.

## Local Storage

Saved calculations are stored locally on your device using the app's local storage. This is used so you can reopen saved estimates and compare scenarios.

The app does not create an account and does not sync saved calculations to a cloud service.

## Exports And Sharing

When you export or share a report, your device's operating system handles the destination you choose. For example, you may choose to save a file locally, send it by email, or share it to another app.

Only export or share a report with people or services you trust. Exported files may contain the tax-estimate details shown in the report.

## Analytics

Production builds may use analytics if enabled by app configuration. Analytics events are designed to measure feature usage and app flow, such as screen visits, step completion, validation patterns, saves, exports, and tax-year changes.

Analytics should not include raw income amounts, deduction amounts, tax file numbers, identity information, bank details, or myGov credentials.

## Crash Reporting

Preview and production builds may use crash reporting if enabled by app configuration. Crash reports may include device and app diagnostics such as app version, operating system version, error messages, and non-sensitive app context needed to diagnose crashes.

Crash reporting should not include raw tax amounts, tax file numbers, identity document details, bank details, or myGov credentials.

## Third-Party Services

The app is built with Expo and React Native. Depending on build configuration, it may use Sentry for crash reporting and a configured analytics endpoint for analytics events.

The app does not connect to ATO systems, myGov, banks, employers, or super funds.

## Data Deletion

Saved calculations are stored on your device. You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. Additional in-app delete and clear-data controls are planned before broader production release.

## Children

This app is intended for individual tax estimate use and is not directed at children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, analytics, crash reporting, storage, exports, or support flows change.
